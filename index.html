About Me

Hello! I'm Mohammed Naseef Bin Mashood.

I'm a Full-Stack Developer with a Master's Degree in Computer Science and a year of working experience in the industry in LAMP and MERN Stack.

While I have worked with both front-end and back-end technologies, I find myself veering towards front-end development more. There is an alluring charm to turning a bare webpage into something visually appealing and striking with CSS, and I want to use it to provide seamless user interface and experience.

An ever-curious individual with adaptability and a desire to learn anything and everything, I'm currently focusing on mastering frameworks such as React and Next.js.
